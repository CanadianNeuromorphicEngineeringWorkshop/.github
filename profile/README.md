# Canadian Neuromorphic Engineering Workshop (CNEW 2026)

**June 15–26, 2026 · University of Waterloo, Ontario, Canada**

> *"The brain is imagination, and that was exciting to me; I wanted to build a chip that could imagine something."* — Misha Mahowald

---

## About

CNEW 2026 brings together researchers, engineers, neuroscientists, and enthusiasts from around the world to share insights and push the boundaries of neuromorphic computing. The workshop combines expert-led discussions, hands-on hackathons, collaborative group projects, and evening social activities in an intensive 12-day format.

**Diversity drives discovery.**

### 🏠 Accommodation

We have reserved on-campus housing at the University of Waterloo. Two options are available:

### Ron Eydt Village (REV) — easiest option
- Shared double room · ~$62/night + 13% HST
- ~$980.84 CAD per room for 14 nights (~$490 per person if sharing)
- **Book individually** email us at [canadian.ne.workshop@gmail.com](mailto:canadian.ne.workshop@gmail.com)

### Mackenzie King Village (MKV) — private room + kitchen + A/C
- Private room in a 4-bedroom suite with shared kitchen and living area
- ~$152/night + 13% HST · ~$2,404.64 CAD per flat for 14 nights (~$601 per person in a group of 4)
- ⚠️ **MKV requires booking an entire flat — you must form a group of 4.**
- To join or form a group, email us at [canadian.ne.workshop@gmail.com](mailto:canadian.ne.workshop@gmail.com) — we will help coordinate groups.
- **Note on booking:** It is possible to book MKV through the conference reservations page with individual payments, but this incurs a 5% service fee and requires that the total number of MKV bookings is divisible by four. We therefore strongly recommend self-organising into groups of 4 via the sheet above before booking.
- [More info about MKV](https://uwaterloo.ca/campus-housing/residences/mkv)

> All utilities and internet included. Accommodation is **not** included in the registration fee.

You are also welcome to arrange your own accommodation in Waterloo — it is a university town with many summer rental options.
